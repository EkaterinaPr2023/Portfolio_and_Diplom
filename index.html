About me

My name is Priadkina Kate. I was born in the Russian Federation. In 2011, I graduated from the National Research Nuclear University MEPhI (Moscow Engineering Physics Institute) as a civil engineer. After graduation, I worked at Rostov NPP. Since 2017, I worked in the engineering division of the State Atomic Energy Corporation Rosatom Corporation (Rosatom State Corporation) on various international projects such as the People's Republic of Bangladesh, the Republic of Turkey and the Arab Republic of Egypt. I have been passionate about data analytics and web development since 2020. I am currently working as a Chief Specialist of Rosatom in the Arab Republic of Egypt at the construction site of El Dabaa NPP.
